Paid Media Agency Workflow

The agency receives a magic link — no login required. They get everything needed to launch campaigns: strategy, targeting, creatives, ad copy, UTM links, and landing page URLs. Read-only. Expires in 7 days.

flowchart TD
    A["📧 Agency receives magic link\nvia Teams or email notification"]
    A --> B["🔗 Opens /agency/id?token=...\nno login required — token auth"]

    B --> HEADER["Header Bar\ncampaign title · slug · status badge · expiry"]
    HEADER --> DL["📥 Download All\nZIP of all composed creatives"]

    B --> TABS["2-Tab Portal"]

    TABS --> TAB1["Tab 1: Overview & Strategy"]
    TAB1 --> T1a["Campaign title + slug"]
    TAB1 --> T1b["Persona cards\ndemographics · targeting profile · psychology"]
    TAB1 --> T1c["Channel mix visualization\nstacked color bars with % + $/mo"]
    TAB1 --> T1d["Budget allocation per channel\nmonthly + daily breakdown"]

    TABS --> TAB2["Tab 2: Channels & Ad Sets"]
    TAB2 --> CH["Per-channel accordion sections\nMeta · LinkedIn · TikTok · Google"]
    CH --> AS["Ad Set Cards — full targeting"]
    AS --> AS1["Interests\nhyper (gold) · hot (pink) · broad (gray)"]
    AS --> AS2["Demographics\nage range bar · gender · location"]
    AS --> AS3["Placements\nfeed · story · reels · search"]
    AS --> AS4["Rules\nkill rules (red) · scale rules (green)"]

    CH --> CR["Creative Thumbnails\nper ad set, sorted by VQA score"]
    CH --> CP["Ad Copy Per Creative\nheadline · body · CTA · description"]
    CH --> UTM["UTM Tracked Links\nper creative, ready to paste into ad manager"]

    CH --> LP["🟠 Landing Page URLs\nper persona /lp/ links"]
    LP --> LP1["Use as ad destination URL\nmessage-matched to persona"]

    CH --> DL2["⬇️ Download individual creative"]

    B --> EXP["⏰ Package expires in 7 days\nshown in header + footer"]

Agency Receives

  • Full campaign strategy + budget allocation
  • Persona targeting profiles (interests, demographics, geo)
  • Composed creatives with VQA scores
  • Ad copy per creative (headline, body, CTA)
  • UTM tracked links per creative
  • Per-persona landing page URLs (/lp/ routes)
  • Kill rules and scale rules per ad set
  • Placement recommendations
  • ZIP download of all creatives

Agency Cannot

  • Edit any content — fully read-only
  • Access raw/base images — composed only
  • See pipeline stages or generation details
  • Use designer tools or Figma integration
  • Approve or reject anything
  • Access other campaigns
  • Access after 7-day expiry without new link
